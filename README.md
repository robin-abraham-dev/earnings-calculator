A web-based earnings calculator for crypto trading partnerships.

## Live Demo

**[Open Calculator](https://robin-abraham-dev.github.io/earnings-calculator/)**

## Features
- 4 calculator modules for comparing scenarios
- Real-time calculations with leverage (0-100x) and trades (1-100/month)
- Indian Rupee formatting
- Dark, responsive UI
- Fixed 20% Partner Share

## Local Usage
Open `index.html` in your browser.

## Calculation Formula
1. Total Capital = Clients × Avg Capital per Client
2. Effective Capital = Total Capital × Leverage
3. Monthly Volume = Effective Capital × Trades per Month
4. Total Fees = Monthly Volume × 0.05%
5. Monthly Earnings = Total Fees × 20%
